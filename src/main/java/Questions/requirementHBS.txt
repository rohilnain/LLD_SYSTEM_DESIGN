                                        Hotel Management System
Requirements:
1.System should support booking of different room types like standard,deluxe,etc.
2.Guests should be able to search the room inventory and book any available room.
3.System should be able to retrieve information, such as who booked a particular room, or what rooms were booked by specific customer.
4.System Should allow customers to cancel their booking- and provide them with a full refund if the cancellation occurs before 24 hours of check-in date.
5.System should be able to send notifications whenever the booking is nearing the check-in or check-out date.
6.System should maintain a room housekeeping log to keep track of all housekeeping tasks.
7.Any Customer should be able to add room services and food items.
8.The Customers should be able to pay their bills through credit card,check or cash.
9.Admin should be able to add,delete and edit a room.
